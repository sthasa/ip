Some of our Extra Co-Curricular Activities

Extra co-curricular activities help in the all-round development of a child. These activities make a child strong at a physical and mental level, which helps the child face challenges in the real world.

Yoga Day

International Yoga Day was celebrated by the students and teachers at Prabahvati VidyaPeeth with great enthusiasm. The celebration concluded with synchronized recitation of shlokas and speech by our Principal. He encouraged students to practice regular yoga to remain fit and improve concentration.

Annual Function

The year being over, the school arranged the annual function the annual feature of the school. On this occasion, banners and buntings. On the back curtain behind the dias was a big banner — Annual Function 2002.Teachers were made in charge of the different items of the function assisted by a batch of students.

Independence Day

15 August 1947, the date any Indian can hardly forget, the day of freedom, the day that bought hope, the day that made India independent seventy-four (74) years ago is celebrated with the same hope, enthusiasm, happiness, and patriotism. Students crafted flowers, badges, little flags and other articles.

Vaccination

Vaccine camp was organized. This vaccination camp was organized for the persons aged 16 years and above in which mainly school students, staff and family members participated and got immunization done. During this all protective measures related to Covid-19 were adopted.
